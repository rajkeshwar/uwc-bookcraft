🤖 Generate Books with AI

✦

AI-Powered Book Creation

Download the instruction file, upload it to any AI, and get a fully structured book ready to paste into BookCraft.

Works with ChatGPT · Claude · Gemini · Copilot · any AI

How it works
1
Download the instruction file
Click the button above to save bookcraft-ai-instructions.md to your device.
2
Upload it to your AI
Open ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any AI assistant. Upload the file as an attachment.
3
Ask for your book
Type your prompt, for example:
"Write a complete book on Core Java using the BookCraft format from the instructions."
4
Paste into BookCraft & Export
Copy the AI's output, paste it into the editor on the left, then Export as PDF or EPUB.
